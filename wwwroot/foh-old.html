Portraits of Children and Their Families Battling Cancer (more images to come). Flashes of Hope is a national organization which arranges photo sessions in hospitals for children and their families stricken with cancer. Participating photographers donate their services by setting up a photo studio onsite and photographing any child and/or family member willing and present in hospital. A makeup stylist also donates time. Some kids who are not well enough to leave their room so we go to them. Then we edit, retouch, and send a photo selection to Flashes of Hope which puts together a picture package that is presented to the families along with framed prints.

To be allowed into the lives of these courageous, suffering young people has been both humbling and educational. They show true heroism not because they have a serious illness but by dealing with it with optimism, generosity, equanimity and forbearance - often fighting through pain. May they all be cured.
